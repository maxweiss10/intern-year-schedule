The gist

Parnassus MICU runs two teams — resident-staffed Blue and APP-staffed Gold. Days: 3–4 residents + 1 medicine intern + CCM fellow + attending. Nights: 1 resident ± 1 intern. Directors: Kathleen Liu (MICU), Wendy Smith (rotation).

Your night week
  • Shift 5pm–6:30am — arrive ~5pm for sign-out; night rounds start 9:30pm.
  • Admit all new MICU Blue patients until 6am, split 1:1 with the night resident; build the morning distribution on the board; sign out at 6am.
Your day week
  • Day team takes sign-in from the night resident by 6am. Holdover presentations are brief sign-outs — full plan gets discussed on rounds.
  • Resident long shift 6am–7pm holds the admitting pager until 5pm; short shift 6am–5pm.
  • Sign out before 5pm to the long-call resident; after 5pm to the night resident. Always check with the fellow before leaving.
Tips
  • The MICU fellow is notified of every admission; the attending is in-house and always on Voalte.
  • ICU transfers: the ICU team is primary immediately, even before the patient arrives — bedside sign-out within 30 min of the MOD page.
  • Notes: ICU writes the H&P on transfer in; on transfer out, ICU writes the progress/transfer note and the floor writes the H&P. Call room in 9ICU is reserved for the MICU resident.
